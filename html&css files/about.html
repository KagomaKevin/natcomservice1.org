ABOUT N@TCOM SERVICE

N@TCOM is a training firm in Rwanda, which focuses on ?
bridging the digital divide and certifying the future by
assisting individual graduates to attain personal, career
and life goals through hands on skills and globally
recognized certifications. N@tcom empowers university/college
graduates to advance their skills and technical experience
required by employers; provide internationally recognized
IT courses from beginner to advanced and to build the
entrepreneurship models among youths to create their own-jobs.
N@TCOM gets down into the process of rebuilding the pillars of
businesses throughcertifying and equipping professionals who
need to upgrade their IT-skills and knowledge, by providing
technical experience required in businesses today and also
our trainee's benefits from our best practices of the “Learn by doing Model”

N@TCOM'S GOALS

N@tcom's goal is to empower the trainee to develop capacity and
productivity by reinforcing the delivery of practical experience
in latest IT and Soft Skills training that facilitate the trainee
access employment opportunities or succeed in self-employment.
N@tcom gets down into the process of rebuilding the pillars of
businesses through certifying and equipping employees (the working class)
who need to upgrade their IT-skills and knowledge, by providing technical
experience required in businesses today. We aim at high quality training
that thoroughly provides up-to-date coursesand Soft skills, as well as
consultancy services at affordable costs.N@tcom works with global
partners who brings in experience and team of experts to ensure
quality of service since its establishment in 2010.
