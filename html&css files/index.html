Manager Operations



Working Hours: 40 hours/week.


Salary: Commensurate with qualification and experience.




Job Details

N@TCOM is looking for a strong and motivated

Software Developer to help build new and innovative

products in a wide range of industries and mentor

trainees to expose them to have real hands-on experience

in the deliverables in our Service Centre.

You will be part of a dynamic, services first

team in a client-oriented organisation that is

passionate about customer experience.

Key Responsibilities

  1. .Hands-on development of the products that your team is continuously iterating on,

  2. .Collaborate with colleagues , product management and systems architecture to develop experimental solutions
  3. .Stay on top of industry trends and best practice customer experience
  4. .Conduct trainee evaluation to find the areas of improvement

  5. .Perform code reviews
  6. .Attend all required meetings including weekly team and reporting meetings

Qualifications

  1. A degree in Computer Science or a related discipline of study
  2. At least three (3) years of practical application of software development and/or engineering experience
  3. Prior experience as trainer/instructor is an advantage
  4. Working knowledge of cloud platforms such as Azure or AWS preferred
  5. Experience with Scrum/Agile development methodologies
  6. Full-stack experience in: JavaScript (ES6/ES7), JS frameworks (React, Vue, Node, Angular), CSS , REST APIs, or equivalent experience) And Backend or Frontend Development experience.

What to Expert

Working with N@TCOM provides an excellent opportunity for career

growth and development in a healthy and diverse work environment.

Our talented and welcoming team will ensure you feel part of our family

to get you engaged on the job. You have the opportunity of building an

international IT career and working with global IT companies.
